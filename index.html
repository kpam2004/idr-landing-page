About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on digital, cyber and technology risk in high-consequence environments.

We bridge academic excellence through UK university partnerships with real-world industry projects, preparing professionals for modern risk challenges.
